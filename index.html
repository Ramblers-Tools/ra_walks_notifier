What it is

A monitoring service for walk coordinators. It runs on our server, watching your group's Walks Manager review queue and emailing the right people when walks are waiting. You only need the desktop app to set it up the first time and to change settings later — it doesn't have to stay open. Access is by request.

Why it helps your group
✓

Nothing gets missed

Pending walks are easy to forget. You get a friendly email nudge instead of a nasty surprise close to the deadline.

☾

No new habits

The checks run on our server, so there's nothing to keep open and no dashboard to remember to check.

◎

Share the load

Notify everyone who helps review walks — not just the one person who happens to log in that week.

Getting started

From request to your first notification.

Step 1

Request access

Tell us about your group using the form below and we'll set you up with access.

Step 2

Set up once with the app

Download RA Walks Notifier for macOS, Windows or Linux and run it to configure your group.

Step 3

Choose your recipients

Sign in, pick your group, and add who should be notified — then you can close the app.

Step 4

The server does the rest

From then on our server checks Walks Manager on a schedule and emails your reviewers when walks are pending.

Questions

Is it secure?

Yes. Your connection details are used only to check your own group's queue, and we don't share them with anyone.

Does it cost anything?

No — it's free for Ramblers groups.

Which groups can use it?

Any Ramblers group that uses Walks Manager to review its walks.

Do I need to keep the app running?

No. The checks run on our server. You only use the desktop app to set things up the first time and whenever you change settings — after that you can close it.
